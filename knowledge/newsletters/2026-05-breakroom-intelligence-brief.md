# Breakroom Intelligence Brief

## The Hidden Cost Of Bottle Queueing

Acme researchers have once again visited offices, observed refill behavior, and written down several truths about workplace hydration:

- when people wait in line for water, they immediately become amateur operations consultants
- one undersized cooler can somehow become the most discussed infrastructure issue in the building
- every office has one person who fills an enormous emotional-support tumbler at the busiest possible time

What smart workplace teams are doing:
- placing hydration stations where foot traffic actually happens
- choosing service plans so filter changes do not become a recurring facilities scavenger hunt
- offering both cold and hot water so one machine solves several small daily annoyances

Acme works best for offices that want a reliable, low-drama solution with straightforward service and setup.

We remain committed to two things:
- better water access
- fewer passive-aggressive sticky notes in breakrooms
