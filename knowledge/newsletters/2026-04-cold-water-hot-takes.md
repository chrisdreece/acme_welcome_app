# Acme Monthly Drip

## Cold Water, Hot Takes

This month Acme Watercoolers is reminding office managers that hydration culture is real, even if no one has officially put it in a QBR deck yet.

Top observations from the field:
- people trust the watercooler that looks maintained
- if the water is only "sort of cold," complaints arrive faster than the service ticket
- hot water unlocks tea drinkers, oatmeal people, and at least one employee who insists broth is a beverage

Acme's position is simple:
- cold water should be cold
- hot water should be hot
- no one should have to jiggle a plastic lever while making direct eye contact with a coworker

Common customer questions this month:
- "Do we need one unit or several?"
- "Can facilities stop changing filters themselves?"
- "Is there a model that does not look like it belongs in a 1997 dentist office?"

The answer to the third question is yes, and Acme is proud of that.
